**Post 3 – Educational (visual: "What is OE Coding?" infographic card)**
Open-ended coding: the most underrated skill in market research. 🔤

When a survey asks "Why did you choose this brand?", you get thousands of unique answers. Someone has to read them, group them, and assign codes — consistently, at scale, across languages.

That's verbatim / OE coding. And it's one of the most time-consuming steps in the whole process.

Soon, we'll automate it. 🤖 Follow us to be first to know.
